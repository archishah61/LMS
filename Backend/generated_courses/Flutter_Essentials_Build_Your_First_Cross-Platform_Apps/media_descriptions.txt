[4:30 - 5:00] Visual: `crossAxisAlignment` options animating on the Column (start, center, end, stretch). Narrator: And `crossAxisAlignment` in a Column controls horizontal alignment. [5:00 - 5:30] Visual: A single colored box appears, then padding, margin, border, and shadow are applied step-by-step. Narrator: Next, we have the versatile Container widget. [5:30 - 6:00] Visual: Code for a `Container` with color, padding, and text. Narrator: A basic `Container` can simply hold a `child` widget and apply a `color`. [6:00 - 6:30] Visual: Animated demonstration of `padding` inside a container and `margin` outside, using different colored highlight boxes. Narrator: `Padding` adds space between the Container's content and its own borders, while `margin` adds space around the Container itself, separating it from other widgets.
